Tom and Jerry

Classic Cartoon Rivalry

About the Show

Tom and Jerry is an American animated series created in 1940 by William Hanna and Joseph Barbera. It follows the comedic rivalry between Tom the cat and Jerry the mouse, filled with slapstick humor, clever chases, and timeless gags.

Main Characters

  • Tom – The mischievous house cat, always scheming to catch Jerry.
  • Jerry – The clever little mouse who outsmarts Tom with wit and agility.
  • Spike – The bulldog who often protects Jerry and gives Tom a hard time.
